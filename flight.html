    Let's Get To Know The 3 Types Of Flight Classes

    If you are a frequent flyer, you are likely familiar with the different categories of airplane seats. While most people are familiar with economy, business, and first-class seats, But do you know that there are other types of airplane seats?

    Economy Class

    Economy class is the most common class. The seats are quite comfortable even though the size of the seats is quite small when compared to other classes. Seats in economy class usually have a distance that is not too wide. In fact, if the seat in front is backed up, it will make the distance with the passenger behind narrow. Some airlines will provide food and drinks for long trips.

    Business Class

    Having seats that are more comfortable than economy class, business class seat types, set a slightly higher price. The reason is, the price paid is very comparable to the facilities obtained. For example, in business class, you will get a seat that can be tilted up to 180 degrees. So that it will make passengers feel more comfortable to rest. There are also other facilities such as an LCD screen for entertainment and a power outlet.

    First Class

    In terms of price, compared to the other three classes, the first class does have the highest price. A series of facilities that can be obtained when you are in first class include the most comfortable seats, wide sizes, and there are barriers to maintain privacy. In addition, several airlines provide shuttle services, check-ins, to a variety of luxurious and complete meals.
